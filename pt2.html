Vistara refund guidelines


Vistara has issued guidelines for flyers who want to reschedule their itinerary.
Vistara has said that if a flyer’s ticket is issued on or before March 31 for travel commencing on or before April 30, will be allowed to reschedule the journey for an alternate travel date till December 31. Flyers won’t need to pay any change fee. However, flyers will have to pay the fare difference.
For Vistara CV members, tier status re-evaluation is paused from March 18 to April 30. However, if Vistara CV members wish to reschedule their travel to a later date, Vistara is providing an offer We offer to opt for an alternate date without any change free.
The airline has stated that Vistara members’ tier status re-evaluation will be paused but they will be able to enjoy their existing elite tier privileges during this period.
