SpiceJet refund rules


Tickets booked before 21st May for Travel Between 25th March till 31st May

SpiceJet will credit the entire amount as a credit shell under your current PNR (Passenger Name Record) for all suspended flights. To make fresh bookings you are requested to visit and at the end of the booking process, make payment using the CREDIT SHELL option. Credit shell will be valid to make fresh bookings for travel till 31st March 2021 for the same passenger. Fare difference will be applied, if applicable
Policies for tickets booked from 1st June,2020. In case of flight cancellations / suspensions SpiceJet will credit the entire amount as a credit shell under your current PNR.
In case you plan to cancel your booking, cancellation charges will be applied and the remaining amount will be refunded if bookings are made after 1st June, prior to that refund will be in the form of a credit shell.
