Airasia Refund regulations

We understand the highly fluid situation caused by the COVID-19 outbreak and the impact it has on your travel with us, flexible options below will be provided for all affected guests:
Convert the amount paid for your current flight booking into a Credit Account which can be redeemed for a new flight booking in the next 730 days; OR
Change your flight date without additional charges to any date before 31 December 2020
You may submit your request with AVA according to the categories listed below
My flight is cancelled by AirAsia
I want to change/cancel my flight
For bookings made through travel agents including online travel agents , requests for changes to your booking must be made via the respective travel agents except for bookings made with Expedia where guests can submit their request with AVA.
